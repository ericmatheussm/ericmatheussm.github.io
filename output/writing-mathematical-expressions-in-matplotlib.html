Writing mathematical expressions in Matplotlib

It is possible to render LaTeX in matplotlib titles, legends and labels. You just need to surround the math text with dollar signs ($) and use raw strings (precede the quotes with an 'r'). Example:

# plain text
plt.title('alpha > pi/2')

# math text
plt.title(r'$\alpha > \pi/2$')
